# Chrome Web Store Listing Template

Title: Verity Companion App

Short Description:
Instantly verify claims on the web with AI-driven fact checking.

Full Description:
Verity Companion App enables quick fact checks of highlighted text and web pages directly from your browser. Get verdicts, sources, and credibility scores in seconds. Works offline for recent searches and syncs with your Verity account.

Key features:
- Inline verification (toggleable)
- Quick access popup
- History and saved checks
- Keyboard shortcuts: Ctrl+Shift+V
- Supports sign in with email, GitHub, Google
