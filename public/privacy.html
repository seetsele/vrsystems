Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 8, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Verity Systems, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password when you create an account
  • Usage Data: Claims you verify, API calls, and feature usage
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device info, and IP address for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our fact-checking services
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send important service updates
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Verification history is retained for 90 days by default, though you can request deletion at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of marketing communications

6. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for payment processing (Stripe), authentication (Supabase), and analytics. Each has their own privacy policy and data protection measures.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@veritysystems.app
