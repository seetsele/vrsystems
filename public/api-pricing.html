Frequently Asked Questions

Which pricing model should I choose?
Choose Pay-Per-Use if you have variable usage or are just getting started. Choose API Subscriptions if you have predictable, consistent volume and want included verifications at a lower effective cost.
How do volume discounts work?
Volume discounts apply automatically to your monthly pay-per-use usage. As your usage increases, you automatically get a higher discount. Discounts are applied at billing time and you're charged the discounted rate for your entire month's usage.
What happens if I exceed my subscription's included verifications?
Additional verifications beyond your included amount are billed at pay-per-use rates ($0.06/verification for standard). Volume discounts still apply to overage.
Do unused verifications roll over?
Included verifications in subscription plans reset each billing cycle and do not roll over. Consider upgrading if you consistently exceed your limit.
How does rate limiting work?
We use a sliding window algorithm for fair rate limiting. If you exceed your rate limit, you'll receive a 429 response with a Retry-After header indicating when you can retry.
