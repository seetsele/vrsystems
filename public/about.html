Built for a world where truth matters

We're on a mission to make fact-checking accessible, instant, and reliable for everyone—from journalists to researchers to everyday citizens.

Our Mission

In an era of information overload and misinformation, we believe everyone deserves access to accurate, verifiable facts. Verity combines the power of 20+ AI models with trusted data sources to deliver truth at the speed of thought.

Our Story

Verity was born from a simple frustration: fact-checking was too slow, too expensive, and too inaccessible. While major news organizations could afford dedicated fact-checking teams, independent journalists, researchers, and everyday citizens had few options.

We asked ourselves: What if we could harness the power of AI to democratize truth? Not just one AI model, but many—cross-checking each other, debating, and reaching consensus like a panel of expert judges.

That idea became our 9-Point Verification System: a comprehensive methodology that examines claims from every angle—source credibility, cross-references, temporal accuracy, contextual analysis, and more. Combined with 27 trusted data sources and real-time processing, we created something unprecedented: instant, reliable verification for everyone.

Our Values

Truth Above All

We are relentlessly committed to accuracy. Our systems are designed to minimize bias and maximize factual precision.

Accessibility

Fact-checking shouldn't be a privilege. We make powerful verification tools available to everyone, with a generous free tier.

Transparency

We show our work. Every verification includes detailed sourcing so you can understand exactly how we reached our conclusion.
