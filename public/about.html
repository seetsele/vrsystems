We're on a Mission to End Misinformation

In an age of AI-generated content and viral falsehoods, truth matters more than ever. We're building the tools to help you find it.

Why We Built Verity

Every day, millions of false claims spread across the internet. Some are honest mistakes. Others are deliberate disinformation. Either way, they erode trust, influence elections, and cost lives.

Traditional fact-checking can't keep up. By the time a claim is manually verified, it's already been shared millions of times. Journalists are overwhelmed. Readers are confused. Truth becomes just another opinion.

"We asked ourselves: What if we could verify any claim, instantly, with full transparency about our sources and confidence level? That's Verity."

We believe accurate information is a fundamental right. We believe technology should empower truth, not undermine it. And we believe that with the right tools, everyone can become a better-informed citizen.

Our Values

Accuracy Above All

We'd rather say "we don't know" than give you a wrong answer. Every verdict comes with confidence scores and sources you can verify yourself.

Radical Transparency

We show our work. Every source, every model's opinion, every factor that influenced the verdict. Black boxes have no place in truth-finding.

Political Neutrality

Facts don't have a political party. Our multi-model approach eliminates individual biases. We verify claims equally, regardless of who made them.

Privacy First

Your searches are yours alone. We don't sell data, build profiles, or track what you're investigating. Period.

Accessible Truth

Great fact-checking shouldn't require expensive subscriptions or technical expertise. We're building tools for everyone.

Speed Matters

Misinformation spreads in seconds. Verification should too. If we can't beat the lies to the timeline, we've already lost.
