Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.
What happens if I exceed my monthly limit?
We'll notify you at 80% and 100% usage. You can either upgrade your plan or we'll pause verifications until next month. No surprise charges.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits?
Yes! We offer 50% off for verified non-profits, educational institutions, and fact-checking organizations. Contact us to apply.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
Yes! All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You'll only be charged if you decide to continue.
