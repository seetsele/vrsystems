Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a verification?
Each claim, image, video, or URL you submit counts as one verification. Our 9-point system runs all checks in a single verification. Bulk documents use one verification per extracted claim.
What happens if I exceed my limit?
You'll be notified at 80% usage. Overages are charged per-verification: Starter $0.05, Pro $0.045, Professional $0.04, Business $0.035, Business+ $0.03. We never charge without consent.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! No contracts or commitments. Cancel your subscription anytime from your dashboard. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
Do unused verifications roll over?
Free & Starter: No rollover. Pro: Up to 500. Professional: Up to 1,500 (2 months). Business: Up to 5,000 (3 months). Business+: Unlimited within 12-month period.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption, SOC 2 Type II compliance, and zero data retention. Enterprise plans include HIPAA, GDPR, and custom security options.
