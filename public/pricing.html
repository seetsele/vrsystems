Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a verification?
Each claim, image, video, or URL you submit counts as one verification. Our 9-point system runs all checks in a single verification.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! No contracts or commitments. Cancel your subscription anytime from your dashboard. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
What happens if I exceed my limit?
You can purchase additional verification credits or upgrade your plan. We'll notify you when you reach 80% of your monthly limit.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, SOC 2 compliance, and never store your content after analysis. Enterprise plans include additional security options.
