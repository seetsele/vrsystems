Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Verity pricing

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately and we'll prorate your billing.

What happens if I exceed my limit?

You'll receive a notification and can either upgrade or pay $0.05/verification overage. We never charge without your consent.

Do unused verifications roll over?

No, verifications reset each billing cycle. We recommend choosing a plan that matches your regular usage.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfer for Enterprise plans.

Can I get a refund?

Yes! We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Contact support for a full refund.
